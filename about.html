Indie Gaming
Global Community 2022

The Global Community brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists of the indie gaming world, affiliate network in a different country every two years. This year we hope to expand our invitations list including organizations and individuals who want ot work with us on indie gaming projects that advance the cause of the gaming community, free culture and open gaming. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of gaming and knowledge - free gaming advocates, Wikipedians, Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant games together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about IG Community 2022!
